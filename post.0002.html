Appointment Scheduling.

  • Morgan Hondros
  • September 26th, 2022
“Everybody in this country should learn to program a computer, to learn a computer language, because it teaches you how to think.”

If that wasn’t enough to grab my attention, he followed with this:

“It’s like going to law school. I don’t think anyone should be a lawyer. But I think going to law school could actually be useful because it teaches you how to think in a certain way.”

When he mentioned law school in the same manner that my father had mentioned for years, I knew there was something to learning programming.

Going forward.

After hearing Steve Jobs make this statement that hit so close to home, I started watching simple tutorials on HTML and CSS. I recall a time in which CSS seemed so difficult that I would never learn it. Looking back, for those of you actually familiar with coding, these are relatively basic languages that are certainly no big deal now.

Over time, I started learning various logic based languages. I’m referring to PHP, Javascript, Swift, and Java. I’ll admit I learned these languages rather slowly while juggling a regular job/life. However, I had fun; and I think that’s one of the most important things about it. As well, I became more organized in countless areas of life. I spoke clearer that I had before. I created step by step processes on how to solve non-programming problems. The improvements in my life, even without having a full time programming job, became evident to me as well as others.

In conclusion

Recently my father passed away. He left a legacy of hard work for others behind. I personally could not have asked for a better example to follow. Putting the loss of him in perspective. I have a burning desire to dive into web development and software engineering to help both myself and those that I care about.

The main purpose of this blog is to show snippets of my programming knowledge for others to view. I also plan to show my personality in my writing and through photos. Personally I don’t know what direction this will take me. However, I am committed to providing a level of excellence to what I write on this page to provide knowledge and to inspire others.

Morgan Hondros
